Follow your training! Poor diving and unsafe behavior will result in expulsion from the club ... diving is inherently dangerous and for the safety of yourself and others, follow all guidelines set out by Divemasters, instructors, officers, and boat captains.











  1. 1.You must be in a buddy pair of 2+ divers for ALL club dives.

  2. 2.If you become separated from your buddy, look for 1 minute and then surface.

  3. 3.No club member may exceed the decompression limits set by their computer or 130 feet.

  4. 4.A 3 minute safety stop at 15 ft. is required for all dives.

  5. 5.All divers must return with at least 500 PSI in their tank. It is recommended that you plan to ascend with at least 1,000 PSI on all dives shallower than 60ft and 1,500 PSI on all dives deeper than 60ft.

  6. 6.When planning your dive it is recommended that you consider environmental factors. Always start your dive into the current, if the current is strong make sure you use the up/down line. Always keep in mind where you are in relation to the exit site.

  7. 7.At least one person in each buddy pair should have a surface-signaling device.

  8. 8.On a boat dive the boat crew is in charge, their rules go! No arguments or exceptions. Pay attention to any briefings by the boat crew, since every dive operation does things differently.

  9. 9.Club Officers and boat Captains can cancel the dive if sea conditions become unsafe for diving.

  10. 10.Penetration of wrecks and caves is not allowed on club dives. Swim-throughs are allowed only when you can clearly see an exit and when there is no risk of entanglement or disorientation.

  11. 11.Intentionally damaging, harassing, touching, or removing marine life is prohibited on club dives.

  12. 12.Divers must maintain proper buoyancy.

  13. 13.No lobstering or spearfishing while on club dives with the exception of approved lionfishing

  14. 14.Alcohol is not allowed on club dives or trips.

  15. 15.Officers reserve the right to deny anyone the privilege to participate in a scuba club activity if they feel the diver could be a danger to himself/herself or others.

  16. 16.Failure to observe any of the above rules may result in the termination of your membership.

  17. 17.For each dive, all divers are required to have

           - Mask and Fins (not provided by the club!!)

            - BCD

            - Regulator with Alternate Air Source (“octopus”)

            - Pressure Gauge and Depth Gauge

            - Weight and Weight Belt

            - Safety Sausage and whistle

            - Dive Computer (on dives deeper than 60 ft)

            - Dive Flag (for shore dives only)


It is recommended that divers have their own wet suit and snorkel (not provided by the club)

Each diver is responsible for his or her gear.  Any lost or stolen rental gear must be reimbursed by the diver. The equipment they leave with is the equipment they come back with.
