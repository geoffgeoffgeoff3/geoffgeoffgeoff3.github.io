The University of Miami SCUBA Club is a student-run organization of certified divers, including undergraduates, graduate students, and alumni. Meetings are every Tuesday night at 8:30 pm in Cox Science Center room 126 (check out the map here if you’re not sure where Cox, or “The Shed” where we meet, are!). Member dues are $40 for a semester or $65 for the entire year. Club equipment includes tanks, regulators, wet suits, computers and BCDs. All members receive free equipment rentals!


Members also get to dive at a discounted rate of $30 per two-tank trip!  


As long as the weather’s good, UM SCUBA dives nearly every Saturday and Sunday throughout the semester. We typically have both a basic, beginner Open Water dive and a more advanced dive each weekend. The schedule is always subject to change. The club typically visits sites in the Florida Keys, Jupiter, West Palm Beach, and Biscayne Bay ... trips have been made as far north as Gainesville and Orlando and as far south as Key West!



If you are not currently a certified diver or are looking for advanced certification please see our training page for more information.


South Florida is one of the world's top spots for diving year round, so take advantage of UM's great location and come dive with us!
