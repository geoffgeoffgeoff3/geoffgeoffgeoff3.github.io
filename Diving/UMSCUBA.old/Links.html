DIVE SHOPS & BOATS

• Austin's Diving Center - A great dive shop on South US1, Austin’s will pick you up if you take the metro to South Miami

  1. • Underwater Unlimited - Just a quick drive from campus, UU is one of the main shops to go to if you need gear!

  2. • Grove Scuba - accessible by metrorail (Coconut Grove stop)

• Florida Keys Dive Center - AKA “Flakeys” - Shop/boat in Key Largo which we often use

  1. • Jupiter Dive Center - Charter used for Jupiter/West Palm dives ... they also have a full shop

  2. • Aquatic Explorers - Charter with excellent service we use for Key Biscayne diving

  3. • Little Deeper Charters - Boat used for deerfield

  4. • Dixie Divers - Friendly, flexible, helpful charter in Deerfield Beach

  5. • Captain’s Corner - Key West

  6. • Ginnie Springs

  7. • Cave Country Diving - the best and closest dive shop to Ginnie!
